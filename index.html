1th Session of the ICE-CREAM General Assembly

10/1/2021

ICE-CREAM General Assembly (ICGA 1) is to decide which flavor of ice-cream is the BEST.
The assembly is held by International Ice-Cream Committee (IICC), and there has been over 70 nations all around the world becoming the member of it by 2021.

Why is this aseembly held?

This is a recording of an ice cream truck. IICC believes that this recording can remind many people of their sweet childhood memories: they rushed to the ice cream truck, discussing which flavor of ice cream tasted the best.
In order to promote the most delicious food in the world, and to "preserve" the rare jubilant moments for human beings, IICC organized this conference. This is the first ICE-CREAM General Assembly, and IICC expects that each country can select their favorite ice cream flavor and give this ice cream the largest discount in their country.
